Welcome to my webpage, I am currently in my final year of undergraduate study at the Indian Institute of Technology, Indore in the department of Electrical Engineering. I am particularly interested in fields of Computer Vision and its intersection with Robotics, Information Systems and Biomedical Engineering. I currently work as a guest research at the Center for Advancing Electronics Dresden (Technische Universität Dresden, Germany) under supervision of Prof Akash Kumar. I previously was part of Dozee Research where I worked on using Vision to detect patterns in human vitals. I worked as a Guest Researcher at NTU Singapore at the Rapid-Rich Object Search Lab under supervision of Prof. Alex Kot on Person Re-Identification. I also had a short stint in 2019 at the Mixed Reality Lab Lab at Institute of Nuclear Medicine & Allied Sciences, DRDO in New Delhi where I worked on Brain Computer Interface and Reinforcement Learning. When I find time I write on Pixel Pie and on Medium for Towards Data Science and The Startup.
